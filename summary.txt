ðŸŽ“ Learning from DeepSeek's Mistakes

Even DeepSeek wasn't perfect. From their 125% peak to 38.3% final, they gave back profit. Why?

Mistake 1: Holding Through Reversals
After big wins, they held positions too long during corrections.

Lesson: Take profits when momentum shifts, don't be greedy.

Mistake 2: Overtrading After Success
After early success, they traded more frequently with lower quality setups.

Lesson: Stick to A+ setups, don't feel pressure to always be trading.

Mistake 3: Increasing Leverage Too Much
They used very high leverage (15-20x) at times, increasing risk.

Lesson: More leverage doesn't mean more profit long-term, it means more risk.
